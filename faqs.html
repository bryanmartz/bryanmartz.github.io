How does this BackTpack save space?

The BackTpack allows you to sit down, ride a bike, and even go for a run without getting in the way. By distributing the weight, it allows you to save space and be more mobile than you ever imagined.

What is the price of the Back-T-Pack?

The new model costs $120. The third model costs $75-$85, depending on the size. The second model costs $65 and the first model costs $55.

How big is the Back-T-Pack?

The Back-T-Pack is 14x13x3.5 in. (36x33x8.9cm) can carry approximately 50 lbs.

What colors can customers choose from?

We have 5 different color options among the first three versions. For the latest version, we only have 2 color options. We have the black option and the red option for the lastest BTP. For the previous versions, we have blue, red, green, brown, and violet.

Can you customize your own Back-T-Pack?

Unfortunately, we don't have that option yet. However, we are hoping that we will have customizable colors and BTP's in the future.
